“The Passion of Saint Sebastian” is out in Hidden Villains: Arise. A queer superhero story about fine art, S&M, and the things we’ll do for a paycheck. 

“The Parachute” is republished in We’re Here, an anthology of LGBTQ+ horror benefiting the Trevor Project. Two men go on a date. It’s not going very well. Originally published in Fearsome Futures, ed. Cody Goodfellow.

 

New Game!

Hourglass Escapes and Fantasy Flight Games present Arkham Horror: The Road to Innsmouth.

What horrors will you find in–and under–this mysterious and seemingly abandoned fishing village? As Stella Clark, solve a wide variety of nefarious puzzles on your computer screen, collect items, and use them in the real world if you purchase the Deluxe Box edition. I am immensely proud to contribute to the Cthulhu Mythos and to Arkham Horror through this interactive story! 

Starring Penelope Valentine as the voice of Stella Clark and Yuri Lowenthal as the voice of Joe Sargent!

 

Escape from  Lovecraft Innsmouth!

I’m the narrative director for the brand-slappin’ new Mystery at Innsmouth escape room from Hourglass Escapes in Seattle! Licensed through Asmodee and Fantasy Flight, this immersion adventure occurs in the same world as the Arkham Horror games and features narrator Cecil Baldwin (Welcome to Night Vale).

As a wise queer POC author once told me, “Go ahead and play in Lovecraft’s sandbox. Just try to scoop out the cat shit while you’re in there.” I do love reinventing HPL’s work.
